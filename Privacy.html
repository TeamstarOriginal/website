Marketing (consent where required; opt-out anytime). 5) Sharing Service providers / processors (hosting, storage, analytics, communications, moderation, payments, customer support). Integrations you choose to connect. Corporate transactions (merger, acquisition, reorg). Legal/safety (lawful requests; to protect rights, users, and the public).
